Effective May 2021 This Privacy Notice sets out the type of information La Milano Pizzeria app collects (or is supplied with). It tells you how the information is held, who we share it with and how it is used. There are contact details for queries about your personal information. All personal data collected (or supplied) will be treated in accordance with current data protection laws. Please read the following carefully to understand how we will use your personal data and your rights in relation to your personal data. What information do we collect and what information are we supplied with? When you contact us, we create a record in your name. To that record we add information that you supply to us. We keep records when you contact us. Data is also collected when any customer or user registers with an application developed by La Milano Pizzeria app. This information is then passed onto the company that the application has been developed for, the Data Controller. That Data Controller can only view their own data, all other data collected from other applications is not shared. If La Milano Pizzeria app wish to demonstrate the data that can be collected then dummy data is used for demonstration purposes. If you do want to receive information about services from La Milano Pizzeria app we will ask you to make this choice when you contact us or upon registration of one of the La Milano Pizzeria app applications. The Data Processor for your personal data is La Milano Pizzeria app. How do we use your information? We use your personal information in the following ways: • To process enquiries and applications. • To provide services to customers that sign up to a La Milano Pizzeria app application. • To provide certain online facilities and/or services. • To allow other organisations to provide services to customers by way of an application that the customer can place an order from. •Personal data is stored in an online admin system that each organisation can use to view customer information and customer orders. The admin system also allows each organisation to get in touch with customers via push notifications. • To carry out market research to help us plan and improve our services. We may contact you ourselves or ask outside research agencies to do so on our behalf. • To produce aggregated statistical information. Who might we share the information with? How long do we keep data? Where you exercise your right to removal of your personal data, we will continue to maintain a core set of personal data to ensure we do not contact you inadvertently in future.

We may also need to retain some financial records about you for statutory purposes (e.g. anti-fraud and accounting matters). The ‘right to be forgotten’ is a qualified right and the public interest test will always be applied when a request for deletion of personal data is made. How can I access the information you hold about me? You are entitled to know what personal information La Milano Pizzeria app holds about you and how that information is processed. You are entitled to ask for your personal data to be corrected where you believe it is inaccurate. You are entitled to withdraw your consent to the processing of your personal data by La Milano Pizzeria app. However, if the processing is necessary to provide you with the service (or information) you have requested then withdrawal may mean you will not receive that service or information.

We will make it clear if this is the case and discuss your concerns directly with you before we stop processing your data. Please make any requests or complaints to: La Milano Pizzeria app Email: lamilanofoods@gmail.com
